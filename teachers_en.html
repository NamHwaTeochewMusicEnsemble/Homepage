Mr Ng Eng Quee

Music Instructor of Nam Hwa Teochew Music Ensemble.
From a young age, he was immersed in Teochew culture, learning temple chants such as Chanheban and Xianghuaban, as well as the grand Teochew drum ensemble of Teochew, China.
He studied the pipa under teachers Yang Haoran and Qi Zhaoming, and in 1978 joined the Chinese Arts National Music Ensemble.
In 2006, he became a member of the Thau Yong Amateur Musical Association, where he delved into the study of Waijiang music and Teochew performance traditions, while also learning various Teochew instruments.
In 2009, he organized a large-scale comprehensive Teochew cultural performance, featuring Teochew grand drums, small drums, flute suites, string-poetry music, temple music, Waijiang music, and excerpts from Teochew opera.
In 2010, he also led a troupe to Shantou (Swatow) to record a complete set of Teochew Buddhist chants.

Mr Li Tingfeng

Mr Li is from Puning (county of Teochew), Guangdong Province, China.
He was graduated from Jieyang Artistic school, studying Teochew instruments from Wu Xudong including Erxian, Suona, and other traditional instruments.
He was the former musician of China Puning Teochew Opera Troupe, and is now the music teacher of Nam Hwa Teochew Music Ensemble in Singapore.
